School Song:

Arise glad hearts, swell fort a song of praise

With one accord, give thanks for this our school

Our hopes are kindled, we now see brigher days

In which to thrive and florish; our fears as our footstool

Challenges will come but we will be ready

Our vision poised and mind calm and steady

We'll be prepared to meet every test

We'll be prepared to give of our best

We'll be prepared to meet every quest

Through educacion total, para personajes totales


Our country calls, we will respond with joy

To take our place, our services to give

Our hands then trained, our skill we will employ

To build and guard Jamaica as long as we shall live

For within these walls, our minds trained to conquer

And on these fields, our brawn growing stronger

We are being prepared to meet every test

We are being prepared to give of our best

We are being prepared to meet every quest

Through educacion total, para personajes totales


Over the seas, in the distance lands we hear

Of places where the work of justice sway

Millions die, and million live in fear

Schoolmates we will teach them, to live a better way

Our way have chanded, we're touched by the master

Our wills are stronger, our love much vaster

We have been prepared to meet every test

We have been prepared to give of our best

We have been prepared to meet every quest

Through educacion total, para personajes totales


So now we say, may this our school stand firm

May truth and love her guide and watchword be

Discord and ill be trodden like a worm

While peace and joy and beauty flow from her like a sea

Until all around on lips and far near

One grand chorus rings out loudly and clear

We are truly geared to meet every test

We are truly geared to give of our best

We are truly geared to meet every quest

With educacion total, para personajes totales


Written and Composed by Mr. Sydney Modest, Former Art & Craft Teacher of Bridgeport High School
